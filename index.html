A bit about me

Currently an undergraduate at the University of Washington, I'm pursuing a major in Computer Science and minor in Education, Learning, and Society.

I'm passionate about leveraging my skills, experiences, and critical lenses to address educational and social inequities through tech, curriculum development, teaching, and thought-provoking questions.
